Dachshunds

Dachshunds are cleaver yet mischievous annimals. They are loving and playfull and think they are big dogs which just makes them even cuter. Don't be suprised if your dachshund wants to go on every car ride with you. They probably think they are driving! They live to about 12 to 16 years but never siez to entertain you. Thay have small legs and a long body as you can see in the picture above. With big ears and a long muzzle, they have a great nose and good hearing. They will always play with you and they just want to be loved and helpfull. No matter how stubborn they can be, their expressive personality and their lively energy makes it so you can't stay mad at them.
