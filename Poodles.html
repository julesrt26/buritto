Poodles

Did you know that the word poodle originated in germany as puddel? Many poodles like water so this fits them well. Poodles are very smart animals which makes them easier to train. A few ways to spot a poodle is by their appearence. Poodles orten have long ears and dark eyes. Their coat is actually made of hair instead of fur and is often described as dense but might get matted easily. They also have a distinct, long muzzle. Poodles are also very energetic and enjoy fetch.
